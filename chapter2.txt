 Believe In Oneself

Before the idea for Harry Potter famously came to J.K. Rowling in a dream, the writer was a single mother struggling to pay her rent. Rowling battled 
depression and other obstacles. Even in those days, she had the belief that her novel would be successful. From no one to becoming one of the most 
successful female writers in recent history, as well as one of the most beloved British authors of all time, she has proved that believe system works.
